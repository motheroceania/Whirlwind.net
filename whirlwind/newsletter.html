post-hiatus, new site name,+ more

hello! so i took a little 2 month break just to get my mind off of obsessively coding this site back to back and focus on other things, now the coding fixation is kicking in more than ever. so you might see me frequently updating this place to hell and back... apologies.

after that break, i decided to change the site name. i wasn't fond of plastering my old username everywhere like it was some trademark (it felt pretentious, i dont know how to explain it). so i wanted to go with a name that spoke more to the site rather than have my old name be the face of it + avoid straying away from my passion project...

so! i decided renaming my child whirlwind. it feels much fitting for the site and this sort of "vision" i have for it in my head. plus, its a title of one of my favorite songs by my beloved band Beauty's Confusion.

i updated the index, and main pages a little bit just for fun. and created a new theme on the music page because woohoo frank's got a björk album to be annoying about.
